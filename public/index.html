Typing Battle

About the Creator

Hi, I'm Chayan, and Grok helped me create Typing Battle—a real-time multiplayer typing competition game. With Typing Battle, you can challenge your friends to a fast-paced typing battle. Simply create a game, share the link, and compete in real-time to see who types the fastest. At the end of each match, the results display everyone’s Words Per Minute (WPM), declaring the ultimate typing champion. Visit our website to experience the thrill of competitive typing and improve your speed while having fun!
